The RetroList

Welcome to The RetroList

As the retrocommunity grew so much, I felt the need to put together a website list of internet trustworthy and reliable source of hardware, software and general information, and decided to made it public so everyone can benefit from it. This is a personal work in progress. No particular order in the list is followed. Nobody paid or sponsored me to be in the list. Feedback is welcome at my Twitter DM inbox @mluna0101 .

Last updated: 7/30/2021
